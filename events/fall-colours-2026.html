Join Us for the Ride

Each September since 2021, the brothers of N.E.W.S. have saddled up for our annual Fall Colours Ride — one of the most beloved events on our chapter calendar. For 2026 we invite all brothers, associates, and their families to join us for another unforgettable weekend through the most spectacular riding country in the province.

We chart a course through northern Ontario's finest backroads: the sweeping curves of Algoma Country's Tail of the Dragon, the rugged shores of Lake Huron, and the winding roads of the Sudbury basin and beyond. The leaves, the landscape, and the camaraderie of brothers on the road make this a ride worth planning for.

Whether you've ridden with us before or this is your first time, you are welcome. Route details, meeting points, and timing will be confirmed closer to the date — watch this page for updates or reach out to us directly.
