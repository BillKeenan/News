Who We Are

Brothers of the Square & Throttle

The North Eastern Widows Sons (N.E.W.S.) is a chapter of the Widows Sons Ontario — a motorcycle riding association comprised exclusively of Master Masons and their families. We are brothers united by a shared love of the open road and the timeless principles of Freemasonry: brotherly love, relief, and truth.

Riding together across north eastern Ontario, we carry those principles with us on every mile. Our chapter is a place where the ritual of the lodge meets the freedom of the highway — a brotherhood that extends far beyond the temple door.

We are not a gang, 1% club, or MC, nor do we intend disrespect to any. We are proud Masons who ride, and proud riders who are Masons.
