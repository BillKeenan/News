Annual Tradition

Fall Colours Ride

Each September since 2021, the brothers of N.E.W.S. saddle up for our annual Fall Colours Ride — a celebration of autumn in northern Ontario. We chart a course through some of the most spectacular riding country in the province: the sweeping curves of Algoma Country's Tail of the Dragon, the shores of Lake Huron, and the winding backroads of the Sudbury basin and beyond.

The colours, the roads, and the company make this one of the most anticipated events on our chapter calendar. All brothers and their families are welcome.
